Enjoying Birds and Butterflies as a Family

Backyard Fun!

Enjoying butterflies and birds in the backyard is easy. Kids naturally want to be outside all day and after these tremendously cold and boring Iowa winters, we easily spend 12-14 hours a day outside from May to October. You'll notice in the video my son releasing some of his butterflies. We put cotton balls, soaked in gatorade, in a small container inside the butterfly house for the butterflies to drink while they were gaining wing strength. Being outside is so important to us. Our 6 month at the time is whining in the video for full family-time effect. It's not always going to be picture perfect but we sure do enjoy it! Some of the things we do as a family are:

  • Fill bird feeders
  • Use kiddy pool so birds have water too
  • Plant easy to enjoy flowers like zinnas, sunflowers, and marigolds
  • Ride bikes daily trying to find new things in nature
