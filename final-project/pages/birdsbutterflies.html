Common Birds and Butterflies in Cedar Rapids

Birds of Cedar Rapids

Cedar Rapids really is a paradise for nature and our numerous parks, trees, green spaces, and appreciation for nature have allowed birds, butterflies, and small animals to thrive in their natural habitat. The beauty of nature can be seen in all four seasons- even in the dead of winter red cardinals can be seen in the snow and robins are enjoying worms as soon as the snow barely begins to melt. A few of the common birds that can be enjoyed in town are:

  • American Goldfinch
  • Cardinal
  • Hummingbirds
  • Wrens
  • Chickadees


Butterflies of Cedar Rapids
